How to use

  1. Drag&drop this link to the bookmarks bar of your browser.
  2. or
  3. Create new bookmark in your browser with this link : 
    javascript:(function()%7Bdocument.body.appendChild(document.createElement('script')).src='https://raw.github.com/Thomaseustache/whyd-shuffle/master/script.js?'+(new%20Date()).getTime();;%7D)();
  4. go to a whyd playlist, ex: this one
  5. wait a few seconds for the tracks to re-order...
  6. hit the play button, and rediscover your playlist in a different order! :-)
